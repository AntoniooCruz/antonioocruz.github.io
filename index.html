HungryGames

Hungry Games, takes us to the year 2050, where 12 billion people are estimated to be living on the planet Earth, in which overpopulation and less access to food resources is expected. As a plant scientist, players will have to learn how to use CRISPR-Cas9 in order to change genes of different crops in order to stop the world population from starving.

This project was my Master's dissertation in which I worked closely with investigators and researchers to develop a game for mobile in Unity for science communication about CRISPR, make sure you see the project and dissertation on GitHub

Bender's Quest

A Bender's Quest presents the player with a singleplayer puzzle experience, where he has to solve several enigmas in order to advance towards the final goal. Together with a misterious and mythical storytelling about a very peculiar village, the player is given the opportunity to immerse himself in a quest to understand the main character's past.

This was an FPS 3D Puzzle Game developed in Unity where I was team leader with a team of programmers, a 3D Modeler, and 2 sound designers make sure to check the game's page

BombinMan

BombinMan is a multiplayer game developed in my second year of university, where the players connect their mobile to a computer and the mobile device serves as a remote. The is a rework on the classic bomberman games with a couple of added powerups and different style.

In this project many common design patters were used and we tried to implement a couple of mobile feautures, like the phone's vibration on death, or a power up that stuns the player that requires him to shake the phone to recover. Make sure you check out the project and its documentation.

Gotta Go FEUP

This was a quick project done in a month in Unity.

Inspired on an a level from an old game called Sonic the Hedgehog 2, where the goal was only to dodge obstacles. We created a side-scroller shooter which gives the player the same nostalgic “Sonic themed feeling” but with completely different game mechanics and goal. The player controls a plane which initially shoots weak slow bullets and his goal is to get the highest score by surviving the longest and destroying as many enemies as possible including the final boss. To do so he can collect power ups which increase his power and rings to regain health.

Make sure you check out the GitHub Page
